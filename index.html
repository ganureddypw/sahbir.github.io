I'm Sahbir Kohli, a results-oriented operational leader based in Bengaluru, India. With over 13 years of experience across India's most dynamic edtech companies, I specialize in building scalable systems, leading cross-functional teams, and turning ambitious ideas into measurable outcomes.

Whether it's launching new digital products, streamlining business verticals, or building strategic partnerships, I bring structure, urgency, and a deep commitment to excellence.
